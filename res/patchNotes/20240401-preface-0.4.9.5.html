Hello again!

First of all, I'm so sorry for how long it's taken me to get this version out. I went through some bad times over the past several months, and I let myself sink into a self-destructive state of mind in which I found it impossible to do pretty much anything. I feel very guilty about the lack of updates and terrible communication, and I hope that I can make it up to you with more consistent updates going forwards.

So, as for this update, I've finished implementing the core mechanics for the new doll race, and added the ability to buy dolls from Lovienne's Luxuries. I also used the framework which I added to support inanimate doll behaviour in sex to implement 'sleep sex' mechanics. I've also added a few other minor things and fixed some bugs.

There are so many things which I wanted to add for this update, but as I've only just got back into the swing of things I simply ran out of time in which to implement them. The doll 'command' immobility mechanics in particular were quite time-consuming to implement, as I had to add support for a non-reactive character to all sex actions and quick sex descriptions. I particularly wanted to get a lot more bugs fixed for this version, and although I've done extensive testing on the new mechanics, I am a little worried that I didn't thoroughly hunt down all potential bugs. As such, if there are any major bugs in this version I will get a hotfix released by the end of the week.

Thank you all for your support, concern, and kind comments during the past few months. Although I failed to communicate with you all, I did lurk and read what a lot of you were saying on discord and elsewhere.

Finally, I know that this update is a little thin considering how much time has passed since the last update. Please consider this release the start of a return to a more frequent update schedule, and I'll make sure to get a decent amount of content added within the next couple of updates.

See you again soon!


Please note: You can transfer your saved games, exported characters, saved enchantments, and transformation presets from previous versions into new versions simply by copying the contents of your 'data' folder into the new version's 'data' folder!

Thank you all for playing Lilith's Throne, and a very big thank you to all of you who support development by reporting bugs, making PRs, or backing me on SubscribeStar!
